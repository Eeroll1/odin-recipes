Finnish Macaroni Casserole

Home

Description

A staple in Finnish homes, this dish is basically just macaroni and minced meat, usually beef, and an egg and milk mixture. The whole thing is baked in an oven. Often served with ketchup. There are many ways to make this dish, here is one of them. Feel free to add any spices you wish

Ingredients

  • 400g of macaroni
  • 400g of minced beef
  • 1 onion
  • Butter
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • Black pepper
  • 700ml of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Optional: grated cheese on top

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni about halfway to fully cooked
  2. Chop the onion and sauté in a pan in butter
  3. Brown the minced beef. Combine with onion
  4. Pour the macaroni and the meat into a baking dish
  5. Whisk the eggs into the milk. Pour into the dish. If needed, add milk until the macaroni-meat mixture is covered
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top
  7. Bake in the middle of the oven at 200 degrees Celsius for 40 minutes
  8. Let sit for 10 minutes
